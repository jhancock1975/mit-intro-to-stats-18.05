2 Maximum likelihood estimator

According to [2] the maximum likelihood estimate for the end points a, and b of the interval for a set of numbers that are values of a random variable that follows uniform distribution is the maximum and minimum values in the set.

The data we have shows the uniformly distributed random varaible to take values, 1,3,7. Furthermore, Orloff and Bloom tell us that the random variable has values from 1 to n in this problem.

Therefore the maximum likelihood estimate for n is 7.
